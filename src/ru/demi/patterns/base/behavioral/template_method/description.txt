Pattern: Template method

Goal:
Defining of class basis and creating of possibility to subclasses to redefine parts.

Using:
Define class basis and allow subclasses to redefine some parts not changing its structure on the whole.

Examples of using:
- for single-entry using of different parts of class, leaving behavior realization to subclasses;
- localization common behavior for all subclasses in parent class;
- management of subclasses extension.
